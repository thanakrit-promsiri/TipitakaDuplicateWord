   Vivaravaggo 
                  
     Vivaratha    imāsaṃ dvāraṃ nagaraṃ pavisituṃ mayā  
 Aruṇarājassa sihena susiṭṭhena surakkhitaṃ nandisenena    
  
                  
     Jayo kaliṅgānaṃ asayhasāhinaṃ 
 Parājayo anayo assakānaṃ  
 Icceva te bhāsitaṃ brahmacāri 
 Na ujajubhūtā vitathaṃ bhaṇanti 
  
                  
     Devā musāvādamupātivattā 
 Saccaṃ    dhanaṃ paramaṃ tesu sakka  
 Taṃ te musāhāsitaṃ devarāja 
 Kiṃ vāpaṭicca maghavā mahinda  
  
     Nanu te sutaṃ burāhmaṇa bhaññamāne 
 Dvo na issanti purisaparakkamassa  
 Damo samādhi manaso adejjho    
 Avyaggatā nikkhamanañca kāle  
 Daḷhañca viriyaṃ purisaparakkamo ca 
 Teneva āsi vijayo assakānanti  
   Cullakāliṅgajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Adeyyesu dadaṃ dānaṃ deyyesu nappavecchati    
 Āpāsu    vyasanaṃ patto sahāyaṃ nādhigacchati  
  
     Nādeyyesu dadaṃ dānaṃ deyyesu yo pavecchati  
 Āpāsu vyasanaṃ patto sahāyamadhigacchati  
  
   Civaratha imāsaṃ davāraṃ nagaraṃ pavisantu aruṇarājassa sihenasusanethana   
 surakkhiṃ nandi senena syā civaratha imāsaṃ vāraṃ nagarantaṃ pavisituṃ   machasaṃ  
   Susiṭṭhena   nandisenena   machasaṃ 
   Saccaṃ tatha  pemakaraṃnu sakka   machasaṃ saccaṃ tataṃ paramaṃ karannu sakka   syā 
   Ahejjo   machasaṃ   Na pavecchati   machasaṃ   Āvāsu byasanaṃ   machasaṃ  
  
                     
     Saññogasambhogavisesadassanaṃ  
 Anariyadhammesu saṭhesu nasasti  
 Katañca ariyesu ca añajasesu ca 
 Mahapphalaṃ hoti aṇumpi    tādisu  
  
     Yo pubbe katakalyāṇo akā loke sudukkaraṃ  
 Pacchā kayirā na vā kayārā accantaṃ pujanārahoti  
   Mahāassārohajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Anuttare    kāmaguṇe samiddhe 
 Bhutvāna pubbe vasi    ekarājā  
 Sodāni dugge narakambhi khitto 
 Nappajahe vaṇṇabalaṃ purāṇaṃ  
  
     Pubbeva khanti ca tapo ca mayhaṃ 
 Sampatthitā dababasenā    ahosi  
 Taṃdāni laddhāna kathannu rājā 
 Jahe ahaṃ vaṇṇabalaṃ purāṇaṃ  
  
     Sabbaṃ kireva    pariniṭṭhitāni 
 Yasassinaṃ paññavantaṃ    visayha  
 Yaso ca laddhā purimaṃ    uḷāraṃ 
 Nappajahe vaṇṇabalaṃ purāṇaṃ  
  
     Panujja dukkhena sukhaṃ janinda 
 Sukhena cā dukkhamasayhasāhi  
 Ubhayattha santo    abhinibbutattā 
 Sukhe ca dukkhe ca bhavanti tulyāti 
   Ekarājajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Imāni maṃ daddara tāpayanti 
 Vācāduruttāni nussaloke  
 Maṇḍukabhakkhā udakantasevi 
 Āsivisaṃ    maṃ avisā sapanti  
  
   Aṇumapi tādisu   machasaṃ   Anuttaro   machasaṃ   Vasi   machasaṃ 
   Dubbhisena   machasaṃ syā   Sabbākirevaṃ   machasaṃ sabbākireva   syā 
   Paññavataṃ   si   Purisa   machasaṃ tthā   si 
   Āsivasaṃ   machasaṃ  
                    
                   
     Sakā raṭṭhā pabbājito    aññaṃ janapadaṃ gato mahantaṃ koṭṭhaṃ kayirātha 
 duruttānaṃ nidhetave  
  
     Yattha posaṃ na jānanti jātiyā vinayena vā  
 Na tattha mānaṃ kayirātha vasamaññātake jane  
  
     Videsavāsaṃ vasato jātavedasamenapi  
 Khamitabbaṃ sapaññena api dāsassa tajjitanti  
   Daddarajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Natthi loke raho nāma pāpakammaṃ pakubbato  
 Passanti vanabhūtāni taṃ bālo maññati    raho  
  
     Ahaṃ raho na passāmi suññaṃ vāpi na vijjati  
 Yattha aññaṃ na passāmi asuññaṃ hoti taṃ mayā  
  
     Dujjacco ca    sujacco ca nando ca pukhavacachako  
 Vejjo addhuvasīlo ca te dhammaṃ jahumatthikā  
     Brāhmaṇe ca kathaṃ jahe sabbadhammānapāragu  
 Yo dhammamanupāleti dhitimā saccanikkamoti  
   Sīlavimaṃsajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Tiṃ aṇḍakā ime deva nikkhittā kaṃsamallake  
 Upalohitakā vaggu taṃ    me akkhāhi pucchito  
  
                   
     Yāni pure tvaṃ devi baṇḍu nantakavāsini  
 Ucchaṅgahatthā pacināsi tassā te koliyaṃ phalaṃ  
  
     Uḍḍayhate    na ramati bhogā vippajahanti taṃ  
 Tatthevimaṃ paṭinetha yattha kolaṃ pacissati  
  
   Pabbajito   simu   Maññate   simu   Ajacce ca   simu  
   Te me tameva   sīmu   Uḍayhate   machasaṃ sīmu  
  
                    
     Honti hete mahārāja iddhippattāya    nāriyā  
 Khama deva sujātāya māssā    kujjhi    rathesabhāti  
   Sujātājātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Acetanaṃ brāhmaṇa asasuṇantaṃ 
 Jāno ajānantamimaṃ palāsaṃ  
 Sukhaseyyaṃ pucchasi kissa hetu  
  
     Dūre suto ceva brahā ca rukkho 
 Dese ṭhito bhūtanivāsarūpo  
 Tasmā namassāmi imaṃ palāsaṃ 
 Ye cetthā bhūtā te ca dhanassa hetu  
  
     So te karissāmi yathānubhāvaṃ  
 Kataññutaṃ brāhmaṇa pekkhamāno  
 Kathaṃ hi āgamma sataṃ sakāse 
 Moghāni    te asasu pariphanditāni  
  
     Yo tindurukkhassasa paro    pilakkhu 
 Parivārito subbayañño uḷāro  
 Tasseva mulasmiṃ niṭhi nikhāto 
 Ādāyādo gaccha taṃ uddhārāhiti  
   Palāsajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Akarambhasate kiccaṃ yaṃ khalaṃ ahuvambhase  
 Migarāja nāmo nyatthu api kiñci labhāmase  
  
     Mama lohitabhakkhassa niccaṃ ḷuddāni kubbato  
 Dantantaragato santo taṃ bahuṃ yampi    jīvasi  
  
     Akataññumakattāraṃ katassa appatikārakaṃ    
 Yasmiṃ kataññutā natthi niratthi tassa sevanā  
                   
     Yassa    sammukhaviṇṇena    mittadhammo na labbhati  
 Anusuyya     manakkosaṃ saṇikaṃ tambhā apakkameti  
   Javasakuṇajātakaṃ  
  
   Iḍipattāya iḍippattāya   si machasaṃ   Māsu   machasaṃ 
   Kujjha   machasaṃ   Moghā   syā  
   Puro milakakhu   machasaṃ paro pilakkho  syā   Yambhi   si 
   Paṭikāraka   machasaṃ appaṭikārakaṃ   syā kārassappaṭikārakaṃ   si 
   Yattha   syā   Samukha   machasaṃ    Anussuya   machasaṃ  
  
                   
                    
     Sabbamidaṃ    carimavataṃ ubho dhammaṃ na passare  
 Ubho sakatiyā cutā yovāyaṃ sajjhāpayati    
 Yo ca dhammaṃ adhiyati  
  
                   
     Sālina bhojanaṃ    bhuñeja suciṃ maṃsupasevanaṃ  
 Tasmā etaṃ na sevāmi dhammaṃ isihi sevitaṃ  
  
     Paribbaja mahā loko    pavantaññepi pāṇino  
 Mā    taṃ adhammo ācarito asmā kumbhamivābhidā  
  
     Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ dhanalāhañca brāhmaṇa  
 Yā vutti vinipātena adhammacaraṇena vāti  
   Chavakajātakaṃ    
  
                   
     Sasamuddapariyāyaṃ mahiṃ sāgarakuṇḍalaṃ 
 Na icche sahanindāya evaṃ sayha vijānahi  
  
     Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ dhanalāhañca brāhmaṇa  
 Yā vutti vinipātena adhammacaraṇena vā  
  
     Api ce pattamādāya anāgāro    paribbaje  
 Sāyeva    jivikā seyyo yā cādhammena phasanā  
     Api ce pattamādāya anāgāro paribbaje  
 Aññaṃ abhiṃsayaṃ loke api rajjena taṃ varanti    Sayhajātakaṃ  
 Civaravaggo    paṭhamo  
 Tassuddānaṃ  
 Vivarañca adeyyasamiddhavaraṃ 
 Atha daddarapāpamahātiraho  
 Atha kolipalāsamarañca kara 
 Carimaṃ sasamuddavarena dasa  
  
   Sampadamidaṃ carimaṃ kataṃ sabbaṃ idañca marikataṃ   syā 
   Manatachijhāyati   yova mantaṃ adhiyati   sīmu machasaṃ mattejjhāpeti   yova mantaṃ 
 adhiyati  syā 
   Odanaṃ   machasaṃ syā   Buhme   machasaṃ   Mātvaṃ adhammo ācarito mātvaṃ 
 adhammācarite mā tvaṃ adhammā acari si syā 
   Chavajatatakaṃ   syā   Anagāro   machasaṃ   Sā eva   simu   Kaliṅgavaggo   machasaṃ  
                    
   Pucimandavaggo 
                   
     Uṭṭhehi cora kiṃ sesi ko attho supinena te  
 Mā taṃ gahesuṃ    rājāno gāme kibbiyakārakaṃ  
  
     Yannu coraṃ gahessanti gāme nibbisakārakaṃ  
 Kiṃ tattha pucimandassa vane jātassa tiṭṭhato  
  
     Na tvaṃ assattha jānāsi mama corassa cantaraṃ  
 Coraṃ gahetvā rājāno gāme kibbisakārakaṃ 
 Appenti nimbasulasmiṃ tasmiṃ me saṅkate mano  
  
                   
     Saṅkeyya saṅkitabbāni rakkheyyānāgataṃ bhayaṃ  
 Anāgatabhayā dhīro ubho loke avekkhatīti  
   Pucimandajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Api kassapamandiyā yuvā sapati hanhi vā  
 Sabbannaṃ khamate dhīro paṇḍito taṃ titikkhati  
  
     Sacepi santo vivadanti khippaṃ sandhīyare puna  
 Bālā pattāva bhijjanti na te samathamajjhagu  
  
     Ete bhuyyo samāyanti sandhi tesaṃ na jirati  
 Yo cādhipannaṃ jānāti yo ca jānāti desanaṃ  
  
     Eso hi uttaritaro hāravāho dhurandharo  
 Yo paresādhipannānaṃ sayaṃ sandhātumarahatīti  
   Kassapamandiyajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Yo te hatthe ca pāde ca kaṇṇanāsañca chedayi  
 Tassa kujjha mahāvīra mā raṭṭhaṃ vinassā idaṃ  
  
     Yo me hatthe ca pāde ca kaṇṇanāsañca chedayi  
 Ciraṃ jivatu so rājā na hi kujjhanti mādisā  
  
                   
     Ahu atitamaddhānaṃ    samaṇo khattidipano  
 Taṃ khantiyāyeva ṭhitaṃ kāsirājā achedayi  
  
   Maṃ gaṇehayayu gahesuṃ   syā   Atikamaddhāne   sīmu Machasaṃ syā  
  
                    
     Tassa kammasasa pharusassa vipāko kaṭuko ahu  
 Yaṃ kāsirājā vedesi nirayambhi samappitoti  
   Khantivādijātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Dujjīvitaṃ ajivimbha ye sante na dadambase  
 Vijjamānesu bhogesu dipaṃ nākambha attano  
  
     Saṭṭhi    vassasahassāni paripuṇṇani sabbaso  
 Niraye paccamānānaṃ kadā anto bhavissati  
     Natthi anto kuto anto na anto patidissati  
 Tadā hi pakataṃ pāpaṃ mamaṃ    tuyhañca mārisā    
  
     Sohaṃ    nanu ito gantvā yoniṃ laddhāna mānāsiṃ  
 Vadaññu silasampanno kāhāmi kusalaṃ bahunti  
   Lohakumbhijātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Pharusā vata te vācā maṃsaṃ yācanako asi  
 Kilomasadisi vācā kilomaṃ samma dadāmi te  
  
                   
     Aṅgametaṃ manussānaṃ hātā loke pavuccati  
 Aṅgassa sadisi vācā aṅgaṃ samma dadāmi te  
  
     Tātāti putto vadamāno kampeti bhadayaṃ pitu  
 Hadayassa sadisi vācā hadayaṃ samma dadāmi te  
  
     Yassa gāme sakhā natthi yathāraññaṃ tatheva taṃ  
 Sabbassa sadidi vācā sabbaṃ samma dadāmi teti  
   Māṃsajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Satta me rohitā macchā udakā thalamubbhatā  
 Idaṃ brāhmaṇa me atthi etaṃ bhutvā vane vasa  
  
                   
     Dussa me khettapālassa rattihattaṃ    apāhataṃ  
 Maṃsasulā ca dve godhā ekañca dadhivārakaṃ  
 Idaṃ brāhmaṇa me atthi etaṃ bhutvā vane vasa  
  
   Saṭṭhiṃ   sīmu Siyā   Mama   simu machasaṃ syā   Mārisa   simu syā 
   Sohi   simu   Ratana   sīmu machasaṃ 
  
                    
     Ambapakkodakaṃ sītaṃ sītacchāyaṃmanoramaṃ  
 Idaṃ brahmaṇa me atthi etaṃ bhutvā vane vasa  
  
                   
     Na sasassa tilā atthi na muggā na pi taṇḍulā  
 Iminā agginā pakkaṃ mamaṃ bhutvā vane vasāti  
   Sasajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Matamatameva rodatha 
 Na hi taṃ rodatha yo marissati  
 Sabbeva sarīradhārino 
 Anupubbena jahanti jīvitaṃ  
  
     Devamanussā catuppadā 
 Pakkhigaṇā uragā    ca bhogino  
 Sambhi sarire anissarā 
 Ramamānāva jahanti jīvitaṃ  
  
     Evañcalitaṃ asaṇṭhitaṃ 
 Sukhasukkhaṃ manujesu apekkhiya  
 Kanditaruditaṃ niratthakaṃ 
 Kiṃ vo sokagaṇāhikirare  
  
     Dhuntā    soṇḍā akatā 
 Khālā    surā ayogino  
 Dhīraṃ maññanti bāloti 
 Ye dhammassa akovidāti  
   Matarodanajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Yantaṃ vasantasamaye kaṇaveresu hānusu  
 Sāmaṃ bāhāya piḷesi sā taṃ ārogyabravi  
  
                   
     Ambho na kira saddheyyaṃ yaṃ vāto sabbataṃ vahe  
 Pabbatañce vahe vāto sabbampi yaṭhaviṃ vahe  
 Yattha sāmā kālakatā sā maṃ ārogyamabravi  
  
     Na ceva sā kālakatā na ca sā aññamicchati  
 Etabhattā kira sāmā tameva abhikaṅkhati  
  
   Athava   sīmu   Dhuttāva   machasaṃ   Surāvirā   machasaṃ  
  
                    
     Asanthukaṃ maṃ cirasanthutena 
 Nimini sāmā adhuvaṃ dhuvena  
 Mayāpi sāmā nimineyya aññaṃ 
 Ito ahaṃ durataraṃ gamissanti  
   Kaṇaverajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Susukhaṃ vata jivāmi labhāmi ceva bhuñjituṃ 
 Paripanthe ca tiṭṭhāmi kānu    bhante gati mama  
  
     Mano ce te na panamati pakkhī pāpassa kammuno  
 Avyāvaṭassa bhadrassa na pāpamupalippati  
  
     Ñātako no nisinnoti bahu āgacchate jano  
 Paṭicca kammaṃ phusati tasmiṃ me saṅkate mano  
  
                   
     Paṭicca kammaṃ na phusati mano ce nappadussati  
 Appossukkassa bhadrassa na pāpamupalippatīti  
   Tittirajākataṃ  
  
                   
     Succajaṃ vata naccaji vācāya adadaṃ giriṃ  
 Kimbhi tassaṃ caṃntassa vācā adada pabbataṃ  
     Yaṃ hi kayirā taṃ hi vade yaṃ na kayirā na taṃ vade  
 Akarontaṃ bhāsamānaṃ parijānanti paṇḍitā  
  
     Rājaputta namo tyatthu sacce dhamme ṭhitovasi  
 Yasasa te vyasanaṃ patto saccasmiṃ ramate mano  
  
                   
     Yā daḷidadī daḷiddassa aḍḍhā aḍḍhassa kittimā  
 Sa bhissa paramā bhariyā sahiraññassa itthiyoti  
    Succajajātakaṃ  
 Pucimandavaggo dutiyo 
 Tassuddānaṃ  
 Atha corasakassapakhantivaro 
 Dujjivitatā ca varāpharusā  
 Atha sasamatañca vasantasukhaṃ 
 Succajaṃ vata navacajitā ca dasa  
  
   Kāsu machasaṃ kāsu   si  
  
                    
   Kuṭidusakavaggo 
                   
     Manussasseva te sīsaṃ hatthapādā ca vānara  
 Atha kena nu vaṇṇe agāraṃ te na vijjati  
  
     Manussasseva me sīsaṃ hatthapādā ca siṅgila 
 Yāhu seṭṭhā manussesu sā me paññāna vijjati  
  
     Anavaṭṭhitacittassa lahucittassa dubbhino     
 Niccaṃ addhuvasilassa suvibhāvo    na vijjati  
  
                   
     So karassu anubhāvaṃ vitivattassu siliyaṃ  
 Sitavātaparittāṇaṃ karassu kuṭikaṃ kapiti  
   Kuṭidusakajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Daddabhāyati    bhaddante yasmiṃ dese vasāmahaṃ  
 Ahampetaṃ na jānāmi kimetaṃ daddabhāyati  
  
     Kheluvaṃ patitaṃ    sutvā daddabhanti    saso javi  
 Sasassa vacanaṃ sutvā santattā migavāhini  
  
     Appatvā padaviññāṇaṃ paraghosānusārino  
 Pamādaparamā bālā te honti parapattiyā  
  
                   
     Ye ca sīlena sampannā paññāyupasame ratā  
  Ārakā viratā dhīrā na honti parapattiyāti  
   Daddabhajākakaṃ    
  
                   
     Dvayaṃ yāvanako rāja brahmadatta nigacchati  
 Alābhaṃ dhanalābhaṃ vā evaṃ dhammā hi yācanā  
  
     Yācanaṃ rodanaṃ āhu pañcālānaṃ rathesabha  
 Yo yācanaṃ paccakkhāti tamāhu paṭirodanaṃ  
  
     Mā maddasaṃsu    rodantaṃ pañcālā susamāgatā  
 Tuvaṃ vā paṭirodantaṃ tasmā icchāmahaṃ raho  
  
   Dubhino   simu   Sukhabhāvo   simu   Dadadubhāyati   machasaṃ  
   Khelalaṃ nipatita   simu   Dahakakanti   simu   Āratā   simu 
   Dudadubha jātakaṃ   machasaṃ   Mā addasasu   machasaṃ   syā  
  
                    
                   
     Dadāmi te brāhmaṇa rohiṇinaṃ 
 Gavaṃ sahassaṃ saha puṅgavena  
 Ariyo hi ariyassa kathaṃ na dajje    
 Sutvāna gāthā tava dhamyuttāti  
   Buhmadattajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Kalyāṇarūpo vatayaṃ catuppado 
 Subhaddako ceva supesalo ca  
 Yo brāhmaṇaṃ jātimantupapannaṃ 
 Apacāyati meṇḍavaro yasassi  
                   
  
  
     Mā brāhmaṇa ittaradassanena  
 Vissāsamāpajji catuppadassa 
 Daḷhappamāraṃ abhikaṅkhamāno 
 Apasakkati  dassati suppahāraṃ 
     Ūraṭṭhi bhaggaṃ patito khāribhāro    
 Sabbaṃ bhaṇḍaṃ brāhmaṇassidhahinnaṃ  
 Khāhāpaggayha nandati  
 Abhidhāvathā haññate buhmacāri 
  
     Evaṃ so nihato seti yo apūjaṃ namassati    
 Yathāhamajja pahaṭo hato meṇḍena dummatīti  
   Cammasāṭakajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Samaṇaṃ taṃ maññamāno upagacchiṃ asaññataṃ  
 So maṃ daṇḍena pāhāsi yathā assamaṇo tathā  
  
     Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā  
 Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ bāhiraṃ parimajjasi

 Taṃ devā paṭirupāsanti tāva duraphalaṃ satiṃ

 Āsiṃsetheva so pakkhī āsiṃsetheva    so dijo
