   Daḷhavaggo 
  
     Daḷhaṃ daḷhassa khipati malliko    mudunā muduṃ 
 Sādhumpi sādhunā jeti asādhumpi asādhunā  
 Etādiso ayaṃ rājā maggā uyyāhi sārathi  
  
                  
     Akkodhena jite kodhaṃ asādhuṃ sādhunā jine  
 Jine kadariyaṃ dānena saccena alikavādinaṃ    
 Etādiso ayaṃ rājā maggā uyyāhi sārathiti  
   Rājovādajātakaṃ  
  
                  
     Asamekkhitakammantaṃ turitābhinipātinaṃ  
 Sāni kammāni tappetti uṇhaṃ vajjhohitaṃ mukhe  
  
                  
     Sīho ca sīhanādena daddaraṃ abhinādayi  
 Sutvā sīhassa nigghosaṃ sigālo dassare vasaṃ 
 Hīno santāsamāpādi hadayañcassa apphaliti  
   Sigālajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Catuppado ahaṃ samma tvampi samma catuppado    
 Ehi siha nivattassu kinnu hito palāyasi  
  
                   
     Asuvi putilomosi duggandho vāsi sukari  
 Sace yujjhitukāmosi jayaṃ samma dadāmi teti  
   Sukarajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Idhuragānaṃ pavaro    paviṭṭho 
 Selasasa vaṇṇena pamokkha micchaṃ  
 Brahmañca vaṇṇaṃ apacāyamāno 
 Khubhukkhito no visahāmi Bhottuṃ  
  
   Khattiko   machasaṃ khalliko   syā   Sabbetālikavādinaṃ   machasaṃ syā 
   Samma   machasaṃ syā   Pavaraṃ   simu  
  
                   
     So brahmagunno cirameva jiva 
 Dibbā    ca te pātubhavantu bhakkhā  
 Yo brahmavaṇṇaṃ    apacāyamāno 
 Khubhukkhino no visabhāsi hottunti  
   Uragajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Jiva vasassataṃ gagga    aparāni ca visatiṃ    
 Mā maṃ pisāvā khādantu jiva tvaṃ saradosataṃ  
  
     Tvampi vassasataṃ jiva aparāni ca visatiṃ  
 Visaṃ pisāvā khādantu jiva tvaṃ saradosatanti  
   Gagagajātakaṃ    
  
                   
     Alinacittaṃ nissāya pahaṭṭhā mahati camu  
 Kosalaṃ senāsantuṭṭhaṃ ṃvagāhaṃ    agāhayi  
  
     Evaṃ nissayasampanno bhikkhu āraddhaviriyo  
 Bhāvayaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ yogakkhemassa pattiyā  
 Pāpuṇe anupubbena sabbasaṃyojanakkhayanti  
   Alīnacittajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Yena kāmaṃ paṇāmeti dhammo balavataṃ migī  
  Unnadanti vijānāhi jātaṃ saraṇato bhayaṃ  
  
                   
     Api cepi dubbalo mitto mittadhammesu tiṭṭhati  
 So ñātako ca bandhu ca so mitto so ca me sakhā dāyini mātimaññittho    sigālo mama 
 pāṇadoti  
   Guṇapātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Na idaṃ visamasilena soṇena suhanussaha  
 Suhanupi tādisoyeva yo soṇassa gocaro

   Visati   machasaṃ syā   Bhaggajātakaṃ   machasaṃ syā

   Suhanujātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Udetayaṃ cakkhumā ekarājā 
 Harissavaṇṇo paṭhavippabhāso  
 Taṃ taṃ namassāmi harissavaṇṇaṃ paṭhavippabhāsaṃ 
 Tayajja guttā viharemu divasaṃ  
  
                   
     Ye brāhmaṇo vedagu sabbadhamme 
 Te me namo te ca maṃ pālayantu  
 Namatthu khuddhānaṃ namatthu bodhiyā  
 Namo vimuttānaṃ namo vimuttiyā  
 Imaṃ so parittaṃ katvā moro carati phasanā  
  
                   
     Apetayaṃ cakkhumā ekarājā 
 Harissavaṇṇo paṭhavippabhāso  
 Taṃ taṃ namassāmi harissavaṇṇaṃ paṭhavippabhāsaṃ 
 Tayajja guttā viharemu rattiṃ  
  
     Ye brāhmaṇo vedagu sabbadhamme 
 Te me namo te ca maṃ pālayantu  
 Namatthu khuddhānaṃ namatthu bodhiyā  
 Namo vimuttānaṃ namo vimuttiyā  
 Imaṃ so parittaṃ katvā moro vāsamakappayīti  
   Morajātakaṃ  
   Pagabbena   machasaṃ   Satanti   syā

 Gāmantikāni    sevassu etaṃ mātālayaṃ tavāti  
    Vinilakajātakaṃ

 Suhanuttamamoravinīla dasa

   Santhavavaggo 
     Na satthavaṃ kāpurisena kayirā 
 Ariyo hi ariyena pajānamatthaṃ 
 Virānuvutthopi karoti pāpaṃ 
 Gajo yathā indasamānagottaṃ  
  
     Yaṃttheva    jaññā sadiso mamanti 
 Silena paññāya sutena cāpi  
 Teneva mettiṃ kayirātha saddhiṃ  
 Sukhāvaho    sappurisena saṅgamoti  
   Indasamānagottajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Na satthavasmā paramatthi pāpiyo 
 Yo satthavo kāpurisena hoti  
 Santappito sappinā pāyasena    
 Kicchā kataṃ paṇṇakuṭiṃ adaḍḍhahi  
  
                   
     Na santhavasmā paramatthi seyyo 
 Yo santhavo sappurisena hoti  
 Sīhassa vyagghassa ca dīpino ca 
 Sāmā mukhaṃ lehati santhavenāti  
   Santhavajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Kāḷā migā setadantā tava ime    
 Parosataṃ hemajālāhisañchantā    
 Te te dadāmiti susima brūsi 
 Anussaraṃ pettipitāmahānaṃ  
  
                   
     Kāḷā migā setadantā mama ime    
 Parosataṃ hemajālābhisañjantā  
 Te te dadāmiti vadāmi māṇava 
 Anussaraṃ pettipitāmahānatti  
   Susīmajātakaṃ  
  
   Yadeva   syā   Sukhohave   machasaṃ syā   Pāyāsena   machasaṃ syā 
   Tavime   machasaṃ syā   Jālābhichannā   machasaṃ syā   Mamime   syā mamime   
 machasaṃ  
  
                   
                   
     Yannu gijjho yojanasataṃ kuṇapāni avekkhati  
 Kasmā jālañca pāsañca āsajjāsi na khujjhasi  
  
                   
     Yadā parābhavo hoti poso jīvitasaṅkhaye  
 Atha jālañca pāsañca āsajjāpi na khujjhatiti  
   Gijjhajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Sandhiṃ katvā amittena aṇḍajena jalākhuja  
 Vivariya dāṭhaṃ sesī kuto taṃ    bhayamāgataṃ  
  
     Saṅketheva amittasmiṃ mittasmimpi na vissase  
 Abhayā bhayamuppannaṃ api mulāni kantatiti  
   Nakulajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Upasālhakanāmānaṃ sahassāni catuddassa  
 Asmiṃ padese daḍḍhāni natthi loke anāmataṃ  
  
     Yambhi saccañca dhammoca ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo  
 Etadariyā sevanti etaṃ loke anāmatatti  
   Upasāḷhajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Abhutvā bhikkhasi bhikkhu na hi bhutvāna bhikkhasi  
 Bhutvāna bhikkhu bhikkhassu mā taṃ kālo upaccayā  
  
                 
     Kālaṃ vohaṃ na jānāmi channo kālo na disasti  
 Tasmā abhutvā bhikkhāmi mā maṃ kālo upaccagāti  
   Samiddhijātakaṃ  
  
   Te   machasaṃ syā 
  
                   
                   
     Seno balasā patamāno lāpa gocaraṭhāyinaṃ  
 Sahasā ajjhappanno maraṇaṃ tenupāgami  
  
     Sohaṃ nayena sampanno pettike gocare rato  
 Apetasattu modāmi sampassaṃ C   atthamattanoti  
   Satuṇagghijātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Yo ce mettena cittena sabbalokānukampati  
 Uddhaṃ adho ca tirayañca appamāṇena sabbaso  
  
     Appamāṇaṃ hitaṃ cittaṃ paripuṇṇaṃ subhāvitaṃ  
 Yaṃ pamāṇakataṃ kammaṃ na taṃ tatrāvasissatīti  
   Arakajātakaṃ  
  
     Tāyaṃ pure untamati    toraṇagge kakaṇṭako  
 Mahosadha vijānāhi kena thaddho kakaṇṭako  
  
     Aladdhapubbaṃ laddhānaṃ addhamāsaṃ kakaṇṭako  
 Atimaññati rājānaṃ vedehaṃ mithilaggahanti  
    Kakaṇṭaka jātakaṃ   
 Sanathavavaggo dutiyo 
 Tassuddānaṃ  
 Atha indasamānasapaṇṇakuṭi 
 Susimuttamagijjhajalākhujakā  
 Upasāḷhaka bhikkhu salāpavaro 
 Atha mentavaro dasa unnamatīti  
  
   Onamatīti   aṭṭhakathā dukanipāta   Mahāummaggajātako

   Kalyāṇadhammavaggo 
     Kalyāṇadhammoti yadā janinda 
 Loke samaññā anupāpuṇāti  
 Tasmā na hīyetha naro sapañño 
 Hiriyāpi santo dhuramādiyanti  
  
     Sāyaṃ samaññā idha majjapattā 
 Kalyāṇadhammoti janinda loke  
 Tāhaṃ samekkhaṃ idha pabbajissaṃ  
 Na hi matthi chando idha kāmabhogeti  
   Kalyāṇadhammajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Ko nu saddena mahatā abhinādeti daddaraṃ  
 Kiṃ sihā nappaṭinaddanti    ko nāmeso migādhibhu 
  
     Adhamo vigajātānaṃ sigālo tāta vassati  
 Jātimassa jigucchannā tuṇhī sīhā samacchareti  
   Daddarajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Tāta māṇavako phaso tālamulaṃ apassito  
 Agārakañcidaṃ atthi handa demassagārakaṃ  
  
     Mā kho taṃ tāta pakkosi duseyya no agārakaṃ  
 Netādisaṃ mukhaṃ hoti brāhmaṇassa susilinoti  
   Makkaṭajātakaṃta 
  
                   
     Adambha te vāri pahutarūpaṃ    
 Ghammābhitattassa pipāsitassa  
 Sodāni pitvāna kikiṃ kirosi 
 Asaṃgamo    pāpajanena seyyo  
  
     Ko te suto vā diṭṭho vā sīlavā nāma makakaṭo  
 Idāni ko taṃ ūhacca    phasā asmāka dhammatāti  
   Dutiyamakkaṭajātakaṃ  
  
   Na sihā paṭinādanti   machasaṃ taṃ sihā nappaṭinadanti   Syā 
   Bahutatrūpaṃ   machasaṃ bahutarūpaṃ   syā   Saṅgamo na   syā 
   Ubhajjaṃ   machasaṃ obhabbaṃ   syā  
  
                   
                   
     Sabbesu kira bhutesu santi silasamāhitā  
 Passa sākhā migaṃ jammaṃ ādiccamupatiṭṭhati  
  
     Nāssa sīlaṃ vijānātha anaññāya pasaṃsatha 
 Aggihutañca ūhantaṃ    dve ca hinnā kamaṇḍaluti  
   Ādiccupaṭṭhānajātakaṃ  
  
     Khālo vatāyaṃ dumasākhagocaro 
 Paññā janinda nayimassa vijjati  
 Kaḷāyamuṭṭhaṃ avakiriya kevalaṃ 
 Ekaṃ kaḷāyaṃ patitaṃ gavesati  
  
     Evameva mayaṃ rāja ye caññe atilohino  
 Appena bahu jiyyāma    kaḷāyeneva vānaroti  
   Kaḷāyamuṭṭhijātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Dhanuhattha kalāpehi nettiṃsavaradhārihi  
 Samantā parikiṇṇamhā kathaṃ mokkho bhavissati  
  
                   
     Appeva bahukiccānaṃ attho jāyetha koci naṃ  
 Atthi rukkhassa acchinnaṃ khajjataññeva tindukanti  
   Tindukajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Janittamme bhavittamme    iti paṅke avassayiyi  
 Taṃ maṃ paṅko ajjhabhavi yathā dubbalakaṃ tathā 
 Taṃ taṃ vadāmi bhaggava suṇohi vacanaṃ mama  
  
   Ubhadaṃ   machasaṃ ubhanaṃ   syā   Bahuṃ jeyyāma   syā   Janitaṃ me bhavitaṃ me   
 syā 
  
                   
     Gāme vā yadi raññe sukhaṃ yatrādhigacchati  
 Taṃ janintaṃ bhavintañci    purisassa pajānato  
 Yamhi jive tamhi    gacche na niketahato siyāti  
   Kacchapajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Tañca appañca ucchiṭṭhaṃ tañca kicchena no adā  
 Sohaṃ brāhmaṇajātiko maṃ bhuttaṃ tampi uggataṃ  
  
     Evaṃ dhammaṃ niraṃ katvā yo adhammena jīvati  
 Satadhammova lābhena laddhenapi na ndatīti  
   Satadhammajātakaṃ  
  
                   
     Duddadaṃ dadamānānaṃ dukkaraṃ kammakubbataṃ  
 Asanto nānukubbanti sataṃ dhammo durannayo  
  
     Tasmā satañca asatañca nānā hoti ito gati  
 Asanto nirayaṃ yanti santo saggaparāyaṇāti

 Aññatu oṭṭhanibbhogā nāyaṃ jānāti kiccantiti

 Yaṃ maṃ khāhaṃ gahetvāna tikkhattuṃ anutāḷayī

 Sāsanatthaṃ    na taṃ veraṃ iti naṃ paṇḍitā viduti

   Kurudhammajātakaṃ

 Vasimbha selassa guhāya romaka  
 Asaṅkamānā abhinibbutattā    
 Hatthattamāyatti mamaṇḍajā pure 
  
     Tedāni vakkaṅga kimatthamusasukā 
 Bhajanti aññaṃ girikandaraṃ dijā  
 Na nūna maññanti mamaṃ yathā pure 
 Cirappavuttā    athavā na te ime  
  
                    
     Jānāma taṃ na mayamsma muḷhā   
 Soyeva    tvante mayamasma nāñño  
 Cittañca te asmiṃ jane paduṭṭhaṃ 
 Ājivaka tena taṃ uttasāmāti  
   Romakajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Kamatthamabhisandhāya    lahucittassa dubhino    
 Sabbakāmadubhasseva    idaṃ dukkhaṃ titikkhasi  
  
     Siṅgena nihanāhetaṃ padasā ca adhiṭṭhaha  
 Bhiyo khālā paka jjheyyuṃ no cassa paṭisedhako  
  
     Mamevāyaṃ maññamāno aññampevaṃ karissati  
 Te taṃ tattha vidhissanti sā me mutti bhavissatīti

 machasaṃ syā   Kimattha mabhisandhāya   machasaṃ syā   Dubahino   machasaṃ syā   
 Sabbakāma rahasseva   machasaṃ

 Atthakāmaṃ pavedentiṃ    anatthakāmāti maññati 
 Anatthakāmaṃ satapattaṃ atthakāmoti maññati

 Hitehi vacanaṃ vutto patigaṇhāti    vamato

 Tañhi so maññate mittaṃ satapattaṃva māṇavoti 
   Satapattajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Addhā hi nūna migarājā puṭakammassa kovido  
 Tathāhi puṭaṃ duseti aññaṃ nūna karissati  
  
     Na me pitā vā mātā vā puṭakammassa kovido  
 Kataṃ kataṃ kho dusema evaṃ dhammamidaṃ kulaṃ  
  
     Yesaṃ vo ediso dhammo adhammo pana kidiso    
 Mā vo dhammaṃ adhammaṃ vā addasāma kudācananti  
    Puṭadusakajātakaṃ  
 Araññavaggo    tatiyo 
  
 Tassuddānaṃ  
 Udapānavaraṃ vanabyagghakapi 
 Sikhini ca balākaruciravaro  
 Sujanādhiparomakadusa puna 
 Satapattavaro puṭakamma dasāti  
  
   Pavedenti   machasaṃ syā   Paṭigaṇhāti   machasaṃ syā

   Abbhantaravaggo 
     Abbhantaraṃ    nāma dumo yassa dibbamidaṃ phalaṃ  
 Bhutvā dohaḷini nārī cakkavattaṃ vijāyati  
  
     Tañca    bhadde mahesisi sā cā    si patino siyā  
 Āharissati te rājā idaṃ abbhantaraṃ phaḷaṃ  
  
                    
     Bhatturatthe parakkanto yaṃ ṭhānamadhigacchati  
 Suro attapariccāgi labamāno bhavāmahanti  
   Abbhantarajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Seyyaṃso seyyaso hoti yo seyyamupasevati  
 Ekena sandiṃ natvāna sataṃ vajjhe amocayiṃ  
     Tasmā sabbena    lokena sandhiṃ katvāna ekako  
 Pecca saggaṃ nigaccheyya idaṃ suṇotha suṇotha kāsayo    
  
     Idaṃ vatvā mahārājā    kaṃso bārāṇasiggaho  
 Dhanuṃ tuṇiñca    nikkhippa saṃyamaṃ ajjhupāgamīti  
   Seyyaṃsajātakaṃ    
  
                    
     Varaṃ varaṃ tvaṃ nihanaṃ pure cari    
 Asmiṃ padese abibhuyya sukare  
 Sodāni eko vyapagamma     jhāyasi 
 Balannu te vyaggha na cajja vijjati  
  
     Ime     sudaṃ yanti disodisaṃ pure 
 Bhayaṭṭitā leṇagavesino puthu  
 Te dāni saṃgamma rasanti ekato 
 Yattha ṭhitā duppasahajja me mayā  
  
   Ambhannaro   syā   Tvaṃhi   machasaṃ tvamipi   syā   Cāpi   syā machasaṃ   
 Lokekana sabbena   machasaṃ   Kāsiyā   machasaṃ syā   Idaṃ vatvā so rājā   machasaṃ 
 si   Dhanuṃ tuṇḍiñca   syā dhanukaṇṇañca   machasaṃ   Seyyajātakaṃ   machasaṃ syā 
   Caraṃ   sī    Byagghāpagamma   machasaṃ byagghapagamma   syā    Imassutāyanti 
   machasaṃ syā  
  
                    
                    
     Namanthu saṅghānaṃ samāgatānaṃ 
 Disvā sayaṃ sabyaṃ vadāmi abbhutaṃ  
 Vyagghaṃ migā yattha jiniṃsu dāṭhino 
 Sāmaggiyā dāṭhabalesu muccareti  
   Vaḍḍhakisukarajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Yaṃ usasukā saṅgharatti alakkhikā bahuṃ dhanaṃ  
 Sippavanto asippā ca lakkhivā tāni bhuñjati  
  
     Sabbattha katapuññassa aticcaññeva    pāṇino  
 Uppajjanti bahu bogā appanāyatanesupi    
  
     Kukkuṭamaṇayo    daṇḍā thiyo ca puññalakkhaṇā  
 Uppajjanti apāpassa katapuññassa janatunoti  
   Sirijātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Dariyā    santavassāni tiṃsamattā vasāmase  
 Hañjema    maṇino āhaṃ iti no mattitaṃ ahu  
  
     Yāva    nighaṃsāma bhiyyo vodāyate maṇi  
 Idañcadāni    pucchāma kiṃ kiccaṃ idha maññasi  
  
     Ayaṃ maṇi veḷuriyo akāco vimalo subho    
 Nāssa sakkā siriṃ gantuṃ apakkamatha sukarāti  
   Maṇisukarajātakaṃ    
  
   Atimaññeva aticaññe ca   si   Apitāyatanesupi   machasaṃ 
   Kukkuṭo   machasaṃ syā   Darayā   machasaṃ   Bhaññāma   machasaṃ syā   Yāvatā 
 maṇiṃ ghaṃsāma   machasaṃ yāvatā maṇi ghaṃma   syā 
   Brāhmaṇatveva pucchāma   si   Akāvo vipulo subho   machasaṃ   Maṇighaṃsajātakaṃ 
   machasaṃ  
  
                    
                    
     Mā sālukassa pihayi    āturanta    bhuñjati  
 Apposasukko bhusaṃ khāda etaṃ dīghāyulakkhaṇaṃ  
  
     Idāni so idhāgantvā atithi yuttasevako  
 Atha dakkhisi sālukaṃ sayantaṃ musaluttaraṃ  
  
     Vikantaṃ    sukaraṃ disvā sayantaṃ musaluttaraṃ  
 Jaraggavā    vicinnesuṃ varambhākaṃ bhusāmivāti  
   Sālukajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Nānummanto nāpisuno nānaṭo    nākutuhalo  
 Muḷhesu labhate lābhaṃ esā te anusāsanī  
  
                    
     Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ dhanalābhañca buhmaṇa  
 Yā vutti vinipātena adhammacariyāya vā    
  
     Api ce pattamādāya anāgāro paribbaje  
 Phasāva jivikā seyyo yā vādhammena phasanāti  
   Lābhagarahajātakaṃ    
  
                    
     Agghanti macchā adhikaṃ sahassaṃ 
 Na so atthi yo imaṃ saddaheyya  
 Mayhañca assu idha satta māsā 
 Ahampi taṃ macchuddānaṃ    kiṇeyyanti  
  
     Macchānaṃ bhojanaṃ datvā mama dakkhiṇamādisi    
 Taṃ dakkhiṇaṃ parantiyā kataṃ apacitiṃ tayā  
  
   Pihasi   simu   Āturantāni   si machasaṃ   Vikanataṃ   si syā   Jaraggavā vā cinetasuṃ 
   si jaraggavā avittesuṃ   syā 
   Nato   machasaṃ   Adhammacaraṇena vā   si machasaṃ   Lābhagarahita   syā   
 Macchadānaṃ   machasaṃ   Madāsi   machasaṃ 
  
                    
                    
     Paduṭhacintassa na phāni hoti 
 Na cāpi naṃ devatā pujayanti  
 Yo bhātaraṃ pettikaṃ sāpateyyaṃ 
 Avañcayi dukkatakammakārīti    
   Macchuddānajātakaṃ    
  
                    
     Nānācchandā    mahārāja ekāgāre vasāmase  
 Ahaṃ gāmacaraṃ icche brāhmaṇi ca gāvaṃ sataṃ  
  
     Chatto ca    ājaññarathaṃ kaññā ca maṇikuṇaḍalaṃ 
 Yā cesā puṇaṇikā jammi    udukkhalaṃ    
  
                    
     Brahmaṇassa gāmavaraṃ detha brāhmamaṇiyā gamaṃ sataṃ  
 Puttassa ājaññarathaṃ kaññāya maṇikuṇḍalaṃ 
 Yañcetaṃ puṇṇikaṃ jammitaṃ paṭiyādetha udukkhalanti  
   Nānācchandajātakaṃ  
  
     Sīlaṃ kireva kalyāṇaṃ sīlaṃ loke anuttaraṃ  
 Passa ghoraviso nāgo silavāti na haññati  
  
                    
     Sohaṃ sīlaṃ samādissaṃ loke anumataṃ sivaṃ  
 Ariyavutti    samācāro yena vuccati silavā  
  
     Ñātinañca piyo hoti mittesu ca virocati  
 Kāyassa bhedā sugatiṃ upapajjati    sīlavāti  
    Sīlavīmaṃsajātakaṃ

   Tumbhavaggo 
     Sabbakāmaddaṃ kumbhaṃ kuṭaṃ    laddhāna dhuttako  
 Yāva so    anupāleti tāva so sukhamedhati

 Tato naggo ca pottho ca pacchā bālo vibaññati 
  
     Evameva yo dhanaṃ laddhā amattā paribhuñjati  
 Pacchā tapati dummedho kūṭaṃ hintova dhuttakoti  
   Bhadraḷaṭajātakaṃ    
  
                    
     Bārāṇassaṃ    mahārāja kākarājā nivāsiko    
 Asitiyā sahassehi supatto parivārito  
  
     Tasasa dohaḷini bhariyā suphassā macchamicachati    
 Rañño mahānase pakkaṃ paccagaghaṃ rājabhojanaṃ  
  
     Tesāhaṃ pahito duto rañño cambhi idhāgato  
 Bhattu apavitiṃ kummi nāsāyamakaraṃ vaṇanti  
   Supattajātakaṃ  
  
                    
     Phuṭṭhassa me aññatarena vyādhinā 
 Rogena khāḷhaṃ dukhitassa ruppato  
 Parisussati khippamidaṃ kaḷekharaṃ 
 Pupphaṃ yathā saṃsuni ātape kataṃ  
  
     Ajaññaṃ jaññasaṅkhātaṃ asuciṃ sucisammataṃ  
 Nānākuṇapaparipuraṃ jaññarūpaṃ apassato 
  
     Dhiratthu taṃ āturaṃ putikāyaṃ 
 Jegucchiyaṃ asuciṃ byādhidhammaṃ  
 Yatthappamantā adhimucchitā pajā 
 Hāpenti maggaṃ sugatupapattiyāti 
   Kāyavicchandajātakaṃ

 Yathāhamajja suhito dumapakkānimāsito

 Daharopi    taṃ na saddheyya na hi jiṇeṇā jarākapiti

 Okkandikaṃ    kiḷasi assamambhi

 Na taṃ mayaṃ sīlavataṃ ramāma

 Komāyaputtassa     bahussutassa  
 Mādāni maṃ maññi tuvaṃ yathā pure 
 Jhānānuyuttā     viharāma āvuso
