      The six books of method  cha nayā  so for given are now called collectively the ANULOMA PAṬṬHĀNA  wherein the Tikas and Dukas are contemplated for the most part as positive or affirmative states  There now follow six books of corresponding negative or denying method  wherein the terms assume as their normal shape a negative or privative form
