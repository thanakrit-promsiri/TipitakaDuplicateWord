 sāsanatthaṃ   na taṃ veraṃ iti naṃ paṇḍitā viduti

      mamantapānaṃ vipulaṃ uḷāraṃ 
 uppajjatimassa maṇissa hetu  
 taṃ te na dassaṃ atiyācakosi 
 na cāpi te asasmaṃ āgamissa

 tāsesi maṃ selaṃ yācamāno

 etaṃ te bhojanaṃ āsi kasmādāni na bhuñjasi

 pahu tattha mahābrahme api ācāmakuṇḍakaṃ

 jānanto jānamāgamma na te bhakkhāmi kuṇḍakanti

      yāva so mattamaññāsi bhojanassamiṃ vibhaṅgamo  
 tāva addhānamāpādi mātarañca aposayi

 tato tattheva saṃsidi amattaññu hi so ahu  
  
      tasmā mattaññutā sādhu bhojanasmiṃ agiddhatā    
 amattaññu hi sidanti mattaññu va na sidareti

      jarūpāhanaṃ khaṇamānā vāṇijā udakatthikā  
 ajjhāgaṃsu ayo lohaṃ tipusisañca vāṇijā  
 rajataṃ jātarūpañca muttā veḷuriyā bahu

 tedaṇḍiko bhuñjati aggapiṇḍaṃ

 suro kayirā surakiccaṃ kadāciti

 te taṃ tattha vidhissanti sā me mutti bhavissatīti  
    mahisajātakaṃ  
  
    bhogaṃ machasaṃ    abhinibbutacittā  machasaṃ  syā    cirampavuḍḍhā  syā cirappavuṭṭhā  machasaṃ    sammuḷhā  syā mayaṃ sampumuḷhā  machasaṃ    sveva  machasaṃ  syā    kimattha mabhisandhāya  machasaṃ syā    dubahino  machasaṃ syā    sabbakāma rahasseva  machasaṃ  
  
              
               
      yathā māṇavako patthe sigāliṃ vanagocariṃ  
 atthakāmaṃ pavedentiṃ   anatthakāmāti maññati 
 anatthakāmaṃ satapattaṃ atthakāmoti maññati  
  
      evameva idhekacco puggalo hoti tādiso  
 hitehi vacanaṃ vutto patigaṇhāti   vamato  
  
              
      ye ca kho taṃ pasaṃsanti bhayā ukkaṃsayanti ca   
 tañhi so maññate mittaṃ satapattaṃva māṇavoti 
    satapattajātakaṃ  
  
              
      addhā hi nūna migarājā puṭakammassa kovido  
 tathāhi puṭaṃ duseti aññaṃ nūna karissati  
  
      na me pitā vā mātā vā puṭakammassa kovido  
 kataṃ kataṃ kho dusema evaṃ dhammamidaṃ kulaṃ  
  
      yesaṃ vo ediso dhammo adhammo pana kidiso    
 mā vo dhammaṃ adhammaṃ vā addasāma kudācananti  
     puṭadusakajātakaṃ  
 araññavaggo   tatiyo 
  
 tassuddānaṃ  
 udapānavaraṃ vanabyagghakapi 
 sikhini ca balākaruciravaro  
 sujanādhiparomakadusa puna 
 satapattavaro puṭakamma dasāti  
  
    pavedenti  machasaṃ syā     paṭigaṇhāti  machasaṃ  syā 
    vā  machasaṃ  syā    kovidā  syā    kiṃ diso  machasaṃ    udapānavaggo  syā  
  
               
    abbhantaravaggo 
      abbhantaraṃ   nāma dumo yassa dibbamidaṃ phalaṃ  
 bhutvā dohaḷini nārī cakkavattaṃ vijāyati  
  
      tañca   bhadde mahesisi sā cā   si patino siyā  
 āharissati te rājā idaṃ abbhantaraṃ phaḷaṃ  
  
              
      bhatturatthe parakkanto yaṃ ṭhānamadhigacchati  
 suro attapariccāgi labamāno bhavāmahanti  
    abbhantarajātakaṃ  
  
              
      seyyaṃso seyyaso hoti yo seyyamupasevati  
 ekena sandiṃ natvāna sataṃ vajjhe amocayiṃ  
  
      tasmā sabbena   lokena sandhiṃ katvāna ekako  
 pecca saggaṃ nigaccheyya idaṃ suṇotha suṇotha kāsayo    
  
      idaṃ vatvā mahārājā   kaṃso bārāṇasiggaho  
 dhanuṃ tuṇiñca   nikkhippa saṃyamaṃ ajjhupāgamīti  
    seyyaṃsajātakaṃ    
  
              
      varaṃ varaṃ tvaṃ nihanaṃ pure cari   
 asmiṃ padese abibhuyya sukare  
 sodāni eko vyapagamma    jhāyasi 
 balannu te vyaggha na cajja vijjati  
  
      ime    sudaṃ yanti disodisaṃ pure 
 bhayaṭṭitā leṇagavesino puthu  
 te dāni saṃgamma rasanti ekato 
 yattha ṭhitā duppasahajja  me mayā  
  
    ambhannaro  syā    tvaṃhi  machasaṃ tvamipi  syā    cāpi  syā  machasaṃ     lokekana sabbena  machasaṃ    kāsiyā  machasaṃ  syā    idaṃ vatvā so rājā  machasaṃ  si     dhanuṃ tuṇḍiñca  syā  dhanukaṇṇañca  machasaṃ     seyyajātakaṃ  machasaṃ  syā    caraṃ  sī      byagghāpagamma  machasaṃ  byagghapagamma  syā     imassutāyanti  machasaṃ  syā  
  
              
               
      namanthu saṅghānaṃ samāgatānaṃ 
 disvā sayaṃ sabyaṃ vadāmi abbhutaṃ  
 vyagghaṃ migā yattha jiniṃsu dāṭhino 
 sāmaggiyā dāṭhabalesu muccareti  
    vaḍḍhakisukarajātakaṃ  
  
              
      yaṃ usasukā saṅgharatti alakkhikā bahuṃ dhanaṃ  
 sippavanto asippā ca lakkhivā tāni bhuñjati  
  
      sabbattha katapuññassa aticcaññeva   pāṇino  
 uppajjanti bahu bogā appanāyatanesupi    
  
      kukkuṭamaṇayo   daṇḍā thiyo ca puññalakkhaṇā  
 uppajjanti apāpassa katapuññassa janatunoti  
    sirijātakaṃ  
  
              
      dariyā   santavassāni tiṃsamattā vasāmase  
 hañjema   maṇino āhaṃ iti no mattitaṃ ahu  
  
      yāva   nighaṃsāma bhiyyo vodāyate maṇi  
 idañcadāni   pucchāma kiṃ kiccaṃ idha maññasi  
  
      ayaṃ maṇi veḷuriyo akāco vimalo subho    
 nāssa sakkā siriṃ gantuṃ apakkamatha sukarāti  
    maṇisukarajātakaṃ    
  
    atimaññeva aticaññe ca  si    apitāyatanesupi  machasaṃ 
    kukkuṭo  machasaṃ  syā    darayā  machasaṃ     bhaññāma  machasaṃ  syā    yāvatā maṇiṃ ghaṃsāma  machasaṃ  yāvatā maṇi ghaṃma  syā 
    brāhmaṇatveva pucchāma  si    akāvo vipulo subho  machasaṃ    maṇighaṃsajātakaṃ  machasaṃ  
  
              
               
      mā sālukassa pihayi   āturanta   bhuñjati  
 apposasukko bhusaṃ khāda etaṃ dīghāyulakkhaṇaṃ  
  
      idāni so idhāgantvā atithi yuttasevako  
 atha dakkhisi sālukaṃ sayantaṃ musaluttaraṃ  
  
      vikantaṃ   sukaraṃ disvā sayantaṃ musaluttaraṃ  
 jaraggavā   vicinnesuṃ varambhākaṃ bhusāmivāti  
    sālukajātakaṃ  
  
              
      nānummanto nāpisuno nānaṭo   nākutuhalo  
 muḷhesu labhate lābhaṃ esā te anusāsanī  
  
              
      dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ dhanalābhañca buhmaṇa  
 yā vutti vinipātena adhammacariyāya vā    
  
      api ce pattamādāya anāgāro paribbaje  
 phasāva jivikā seyyo yā vādhammena phasanāti  
    lābhagarahajātakaṃ    
  
              
      agghanti macchā adhikaṃ sahassaṃ 
 na so atthi yo imaṃ saddaheyya  
 mayhañca assu idha satta māsā 
 ahampi taṃ macchuddānaṃ   kiṇeyyanti  
  
      macchānaṃ bhojanaṃ datvā mama dakkhiṇamādisi     
 taṃ dakkhiṇaṃ parantiyā kataṃ apacitiṃ tayā  
  
    pihasi  simu    āturantāni  si  machasaṃ    vikanataṃ  si syā    jaraggavā vā cinetasuṃ  si jaraggavā avittesuṃ  syā 
    nato  machasaṃ    adhammacaraṇena vā  si machasaṃ    lābhagarahita  syā    macchadānaṃ  machasaṃ    madāsi  machasaṃ  
  
              
               
      paduṭhacintassa na phāni hoti 
 na cāpi naṃ devatā pujayanti  
 yo bhātaraṃ pettikaṃ sāpateyyaṃ 
 avañcayi dukkatakammakārīti    
    macchuddānajātakaṃ    
  
              
      nānācchandā   mahārāja ekāgāre vasāmase  
 ahaṃ gāmacaraṃ icche brāhmaṇi ca gāvaṃ sataṃ  
  
      chatto ca   ājaññarathaṃ kaññā ca maṇikuṇaḍalaṃ 
 yā cesā puṇaṇikā jammi   udukkhalaṃ   
  
              
      brahmaṇassa gāmavaraṃ detha brāhmamaṇiyā gamaṃ sataṃ  
 puttassa ājaññarathaṃ kaññāya maṇikuṇḍalaṃ 
 yañcetaṃ puṇṇikaṃ jammitaṃ paṭiyādetha udukkhalanti  
    nānācchandajātakaṃ  
  
      sīlaṃ kireva kalyāṇaṃ sīlaṃ loke anuttaraṃ  
 passa ghoraviso nāgo silavāti na haññati  
  
              
      sohaṃ sīlaṃ samādissaṃ loke anumataṃ sivaṃ  
 ariyavutti   samācāro yena vuccati silavā  
  
      ñātinañca piyo hoti mittesu ca virocati  
 kāyassa bhedā sugatiṃ upapajjati   sīlavāti  
     sīlavīmaṃsajātakaṃ  
  
 abbhantaravaggo catuttho  
  
 tassuddānaṃ  
 dumakaṃsavaruttama byagghamigā 
 maṇiyo maṇi sālukamavhayano  
 anusāsaniyopi ca macchavaro 
 maṇikuṇḍalakenakirena dasāti 
  
    dukkaṭa kammakāriti  machasaṃ syā    macchadāna jātakaṃ  machasaṃ syā    nānāchandā  nānacchanda  machasaṃ    putetā  machasaṃ syā    puṇṇikajammiṃ  machasaṃ puṇaṇakā jammi  syā    udukkhalabhi kaṅkhati     ariyavatta  syā    uppajajati  syā 
  
              
               
    tumbhavaggo 
      sabbakāmaddaṃ kumbhaṃ kuṭaṃ   laddhāna dhuttako  
 yāva so   anupāleti tāva so sukhamedhati  
  
      yadā matto ca ditto ca pamādā kumbhamabbhidā  
 tato naggo ca pottho ca pacchā bālo vibaññati 
  
      evameva yo dhanaṃ laddhā amattā paribhuñjati  
 pacchā tapati dummedho kūṭaṃ hintova dhuttakoti  
    bhadraḷaṭajātakaṃ   
  
              
      bārāṇassaṃ   mahārāja kākarājā nivāsiko    
 asitiyā sahassehi supatto parivārito  
  
      tasasa dohaḷini bhariyā suphassā macchamicachati    
 rañño mahānase pakkaṃ paccagaghaṃ rājabhojanaṃ  
  
      tesāhaṃ pahito duto rañño cambhi idhāgato  
 bhattu apavitiṃ kummi nāsāyamakaraṃ vaṇanti  
    supattajātakaṃ  
  
              
      phuṭṭhassa me aññatarena vyādhinā 
 rogena khāḷhaṃ dukhitassa ruppato  
 parisussati khippamidaṃ kaḷekharaṃ 
 pupphaṃ yathā saṃsuni ātape kataṃ  
  
      ajaññaṃ jaññasaṅkhātaṃ asuciṃ sucisammataṃ  
 nānākuṇapaparipuraṃ jaññarūpaṃ apassato 
  
      dhiratthu taṃ āturaṃ putikāyaṃ 
 jegucchiyaṃ asuciṃ byādhidhammaṃ  
 yatthappamantā adhimucchitā pajā 
 hāpenti maggaṃ sugatupapattiyāti 
    kāyavicchandajātakaṃ  
  
    kūṭa  si syā    naṃ  machasaṃ    surāghaṭajātakaṃ  machasaṃ bhadraghaṭabhedaka jātakaṃ  syā    khārāṇasyaṃ  machasaṃ  syā    nivāsako  machasaṃ  syā    suphassā bhakkhatu micchati  machaṃ  syā  
  
              
               
      koyaṃ bindussaro maggu pavadantānamuttamo  
 accuto jambusākhāya mojacchāpova kujati  
  
      kulaputtova jānāti kulaputte pasaṃsituṃ  
 vyagghacchāpasarivaṇṇo   bhuñja samma dadāmi te  
  
      cirassaṃ vata passāmi musāvādi samāgate  
 vannādaṃ kuṇapādañca aññamaññaṃ pasaṃsaketi  
    jambukhādakajātakaṃ  
  
              
      usabhasseva te khandho sihasseva vijamhitaṃ  
 migarāja namotyatthu api kiñci labhāmase  
  
      kulaputtova   jānāti kulaputte pasaṃsituṃ  
 mayuragivasaṅkāsa ito pariyāhi vāyasa  
  
      migānaṃ kotthuko   anto pakkhinaṃ pana vāyaso  
 eraṇḍo anto rukkhānaṃ tayo antā samāgatāti  
    antajātakaṃ  
  
              
      konāyaṃ   loṇatoyasmiṃ samantā paridhāvati  
 macche makare ca vāreti ūmisu   ca vihaññati  
  
      anantapāyi sakuṇo atittoti disāsuto  
 samuddaṃ pātumicchāmi sāgaraṃ saritaṃ patiṃ  
  
      svāyaṃ hāyati ceva purate ca mahodadhi  
 nāssa nāyati pitanto   apeyyo kira sāgaroti  
    samuddajātakaṃ  
  
    byasghacchāpi sariravaṇaṇā machasaṃ  vyagghacchāpa sadisavaṇaṇa  syā    kulaputto pajānāti  machasaṃ  syā    siṅgālo  machasaṃ koṭṭhuko  syā    konavāyaṃ  syā    ummisu  machasaṃ ūmisu  si    pivatto  machasaṃ 
  
              
               
      ucce sakuṇa ḍemāna   pattayāna vihaṅgama  
 vajjāsi kho tvaṃ vāmuruṃ ciraṃ kho sā karissati  
  
      idaṃ kho sā na jānāti asiṃ sattiñca oḍḍitaṃ  
 sā caṇḍi kāhati kodhaṃ taṃ me tapati no idha    
  
      esa uppalasannāho nikkhamussisake   kataṃ  
 kāsikañca muduṃ vatthaṃ tappatu   dhanakāmikāti    
    kāmavilāpajātakaṃ  
  
              
      udumbarā cime pakkā nigrodhā ca kapitthanā  
 ehi nikkhama   bhuñjassu kiṃ jighacchāya miyasi  
  
      evaṃ so suhito hoti yo vaddamapacāyati    
 yathāhamajja suhito dumapakkānimāsito  
  
      yaṃ vanejo vanejassa vañceyya kapino kapi  
 daharopi    taṃ na saddheyya na hi jiṇeṇā jarākapiti  
    udumabarajātakaṃ  
  
              
      pure tuvaṃ sīlavataṃ sakāse 
 okkandikaṃ   kiḷasi assamambhi  
 karohare makkaṭiyāni makkaṭa 
 na taṃ mayaṃ sīlavataṃ ramāma  
  
      sutā hi mayhaṃ paramā visuddhi 
 komāyaputtassa    bahussutassa  
 mādāni maṃ maññi tuvaṃ yathā pure 
 jhānānuyuttā    viharāma āvuso  
  
    ḍehana  machasaṃ     idaṃ  machasaṃ    nikkhañcussisako hitaṃ  machasaṃ nikkhañcussisake  syā    tappetu  si machasaṃ syā    dhanikā piyā  machasaṃ    nikkhamma  machasaṃ    yo vuddhamapacāyati  machasaṃ syā    daharo kapi saḍeyya  machasaṃ    okkantikaṃ  machasaṃ     komāriya puttassa  syā komāraputtassaṃ  machasaṃ     jhānā niyuttā  si  
  
              
               
      sacepi selasmiṃ vapeyya khijaṃ 
 devo ca vasse neva hi taṃ ruheyya  
 sutā hi te sā paramā visuddhi 
 ārā tuvaṃ makkaṭa jhānabhumiyāti  
    komāputtajātakaṃ    
  
              
      parapāṇarodhā   jivanto maṃsa lohitabhojano    
 vako vataṃ samādāya   upapajji uposathaṃ  
  
      tassa sakko vataññāya ajarūpenupāgami  
 vitatapo   ajjhappatto   bhañji lohitapo tapaṃ  
  
              
      evavemavaṃ idhekacce samādānasmiṃ dubabalā  
 lahuṃ karonti attānaṃ vakova ajakāraṇāti  
     vakajātakaṃ  kumbhavaggo pañcamo  tassuddānaṃ  varakumbhasuttasirivabhayano suvisammatabinduvaro cusabho  saritapaticaṇḍijarākapinā atha makkaṭiyā vakakena dasāti  tikanipāto niṭṭhito  vagguddānaṃ  saṅkappo padumo ceva udapānena tatiyaṃ  
 abbhantaraṃ ghaṭabhedaṃ tikanipātembi laṅkatanti  
  
    komāraputtajātakaṃ  machasaṃ    parapāṇaghāte  syā    lohitā  machasaṃ    samādiyi  si    vitalitapo machasaṃ si    ajjhapatto  machasaṃ  syā
