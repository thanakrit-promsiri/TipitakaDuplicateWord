    saṅkha cariyaṃ 
             
     punāparaṃ yadā homi brāhmaṇo saṅkhasavhayo 
 mahāsamuddaṃ taritukāmo upagacchāmi paṭṭanaṃ   
  
     tatthaddasāmi paṭipathe sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ 
 kantāraddhāna  paṭipannaṃ tattāya kaṭhinabhūmiyā  
  
     tamahaṃ paṭipathe disvā imamatthaṃ vicintayiṃ 
 idaṃ  khettaṃ anuppattaṃ puññakāmassa jantuno  
  
     yathāpi   kassako puriso khettaṃ disvā mahāgamaṃ    
 tattha bījaṃ na ropeti na so dhaññena atthiko  
  
     evamevāhaṃ puññakāmo disvā khetta   varuttamaṃ 
 yadi tattha kāraṃ na karomi nāhaṃ puññena atthiko  
  
     yathā amacco muddikāmo rañño ante pure jane 
 na deti tesaṃ dhanadhaññaṃ muddito parihāyati  
  
     evamevāhaṃ puññakāmo vipulaṃ disvāna dakkhiṇaṃ 
 yadi tassa dānaṃ na dadāmi parihāyissāmi puññato  
  
     evāhaṃ vintayitvāna orohitvā upāhanā 
 tassa pādāni vanditvā adāsiṃ chattupāhanaṃ  
  
     tenevāhaṃ sataguṇato sukhumālo sukhedhito 
 apica dānāṃ paripūrento evaṃ tassa adāsahanti

 āyācuṃ maṃ hatthināgaṃ dhaññaṃ maṅgalasammataṃ  
  
     avuṭṭhiko janapado dubbhikkho jātako mahā 
 dadāhi pavaraṃ nāgaṃ nīlaṃ añjanasavhayaṃ

 mā me bhijji samādānaṃ dassāmi vipulaṃ gajaṃ  
  
     nāgaṃ gahetvā soṇḍāya bhiṅkāre ratanāmaye 
 jalaṃ hatthe ākiritvā brāhmaṇānaṃ adaṃ gajaṃ  
  
     tassa nāge padinnamhi amaccā etadabrūvuṃ 
 kinnu tuyhaṃ varaṃ nāgaṃ yācakānaṃ padassasi

 tasmiṃ nāge padinnamhi kinte rajjaṃ  karissati  
  
     rajjampi me dade sabbaṃ sarīraṃ dajjamattano 
 sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ tasmā nāgaṃ adāsahanti

 tattha dānaṃ pavattesiṃ migapakkhinarādinaṃ

 abbocchinnaṃ karitvāna mahādānaṃ pavattayiṃ

 kāyena vācā manasā ārādhaniyamesati

    candakumāra cariyaṃ 
             
     punāparaṃ yadā homi ekarājassa atrajo 
 nagare pupphavatiyā kumāro candasavhayo  
  
     tadāhaṃ yajanā mutto nikkhanto yaññavāṭato  
 saṃvegaṃ janayitvāna mahādānaṃ pavattayiṃ  
  
     nāhaṃ pivāmi khādāmi napi bhuñjāmi bhojanaṃ 
 dakkhiṇeyye adatvāna   api chappañca rattiyo  
  
     yathāpi vāṇijo nāma katvāna bhaṇḍasañcayaṃ 
 yattha lābho mahā hoti tattha taṃ harati bhaṇḍakaṃ  
  
     tatheva sakabhuttāpi   pare dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ 
 tasmā parassa dātabbaṃ satabhāgo bhavissati  
  
     etamatthavasaṃ ñatvā demi dānaṃ bhavābhave 
 na paṭikkamāmi dānato sambodhimanupattiyāti

    sivirāja cariyaṃ 
  
     ariṭṭhasavhaye nagare sivi nāmāsiṃ  khattiyo 
 nisajja pāsādavare evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā  
  
     yaṃ kiñci mānusaṃ dānaṃ adinnaṃ me na vijjati 
 yopi vāceyya maṃ cakkhuṃ dadeyyaṃ avikampito  
  
     mama saṅkappamaññāya sakko devānamissaro 
 nisinno devaparisāya idaṃ vacanamabruvi  
  
     nisajjapāsādavare sivirājā mahiddhiko 
 vintento vividhaṃ dānaṃ adeyyaṃ so na passati  
  
     tathannu vitathantetaṃ handa vīmaṃsayāmi   taṃ 
 muhuttaṃ āgameyyātha yāva jānāmi taṃ manaṃ  
  
     pavedhamāno palitasiro valitagatto jarāturo 
 andhavaṇṇova hutvāna rājānaṃ upasaṅkami  
             
     so tadā paggahetvāna vāmaṃ dakkhiṇabāhu ca 
 sirasmiṃ añjaliṃ katvā idaṃ vacanamabruvi  
  
     yācāmi taṃ mahārāja dhammikaraṭṭhavaḍḍhana 
 tava dānaratā kitti uggatā devamānuse  
  
     ubhopi nettā nayanā andhā upahatā mama 
 ekaṃ me nayanaṃ dehi tvampi ekena yāpaya  
  
     tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā bhaṭṭho saṃviggamānaso 
 katañjalī vedajāto idaṃ vacanamabruviṃ  
  
     idānāhaṃ cintayitvā  pāsādato idhāgato 
 tvaṃ mama cittamaññāya nettaṃ yācitumāgato  
  
    nāmādhikatthaci    vimaṃsissāmikatthaci    cītatadhitvānamachasaṃ  syā  
  
               
  
     aho me mānasaṃ siddhaṃ saṅkappo paripūrito 
 adinnapubbaṃ dānavaraṃ ajja dassāmi yācake  
  
     ehi sīvaka uṭṭhehi mā dandhayi mā pavedhayi ubhopi nayane dehi uppāṭetvā vaṇibbake  
  
     tato so vodito mayhaṃ sīvako vacanaṅkaro 
 uddharitvāna pādāsi tālamiñjaṃva yācake

 cittassa aññathā natthi bodhiyā yeva kāraṇā  
  
     na me dessā ubho cakkhu attā na me na dessiyo  
 sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ tasmā cakkhuṃ adāsahanti

    upekkhā pāramitā 
  
     mahālomahaṃsa cariyaṃ 
  
      susāne seyyaṃ kappemi javaṭṭhikaṃ upanidhāya haṃ  
 gomaṇḍalā  upāgantvā rūpaṃ dassenti nappakaṃ   
  
      apare gandhañca mālañca   bhojanaṃ vividhaṃ bahuṃ 
 upāyanānyupanenti haṭṭhā saṃviggamānasā  
  
      ye me dukkhaṃ upadahanti ye ca denti sukhaṃ mama 
 sabbesaṃ samako homi dayākopo  navijjati  
  
      sukhadukkhatulā bhūto yasesu ayasesuca 
 sabbattha samako homi esā me upekkhā pāramīti  
  
 mahālomahaṃsa cariyaṃ paṇṇarasamaṃ  
  
 upekkhā pāramitā niṭṭhitā  
  
 uddāna gāthā  
  
      yudhañjayo somanasso ayoghara bhisenaca 
 soṇanando  mūgapakkho kapirājā  saccasavhayo

 sutasomo puna āsiṃ sāmoca ekarājahu 
 upekkhā pāramī āsi iti vuttaṃ mahesinā  
  
      evaṃ bahuvidhiṃ dukkhaṃ sampatti ca   bahuvidhā   
 bhavā bhave anubhavitvā patto sambodhimuttamaṃ

 nekkhamme   pāramiṃ gantvā patto sambodhimuttamaṃ
